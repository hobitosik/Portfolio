The 5th International Innovation Forum and Festival rASiA.com encourages exchange of the latest technologies and experience between entrepreneurs from all over the world

The 5th International Innovation Forum and Festival rASiA.com will be held on 20-22 June 2015 in Moscow, Russia.

Forum rASiA.com encourages exchange of the latest technologies and experience between entrepreneurs from all over the world.

Global leaders will discuss the latest innovations, opportunities for cooperation and investment, will analyze interesting cases, tell their stories of success, reveal important trends and show how innovative technologies can change world and support in development of the global economies of Russia, Asia and the West.

This year's business program will focus on June 22. The key event of the Forum rASiA.com is a unique MESTECH Summit. MESTECH is a meeting point of the rapid growing spheres: Media, Entertainment and Sport, which are closely connected with IT. During the MESTECH Summit leading figures could share knowledge about innovative instruments, learn about promising technologies and also find point for cooperation with Russian companies. Here experts will discuss the main trends of media development, entertaining content distribution and sport events development.

The event's list of speakers includes:

  1. Hans Hyun, Partner of Emerging Asia Capital Partners, has over 25 year experience in various fields of investment banking, especially in energy, infrastructure and natural resource areas (Japan)
  2. Ed Yen, Head of Project Office of the Taiwan Government Information Office, Director of concerts and TV broadcasting for Asia Olympic Games (Taiwan)
  3. Frédéric Vaulpré, Vice-President of Eurodata TV Worldwide (France)
  4. Michael Bornhäusser, Serial entrepreneur, member of the board of directors at several companies (Switzerland)
  5. Benjamin J. Butler, Owner of Edge Capital, Founder of Apotheosis Consulting, Futurist (UK)
  6. Tamerlan Abdikeev, Managing Partner at Sun Investment LLC (USA)
  7. Jerry Yang, Partner at Hardware Club (Taiwan)
  8. Alexander Shulgin, visionary, author, composer, serial entrepreneur and investor, public leader (Russia)
  9. Givi Topchishvili, Founder & CEO of 9.8 Group, Inc. (USA)
  10. Youri Hazanov, Head of Partnership at YouTube Russia and CIS (Russia)
  11. Ian Penman, Lawyer, partner at New Media Law (UK)
  12. Andre Gromkovski, Managing Director Russia & CIS Home Entertainment at Twentieth Century Fox (Russia)
  13. Peter Kharma, Producer, Dj, musician and sound engineer at Np Music International (Italy)
  14. Greig Watts, Producer, publisher and owner of DWB Music Ltd (UK)
  15. Billy Koh, Founder and former CEO of Ocean Butterflies sets up Amusic Rights Management Company, record producer, artist, songwriter (Singapore)
  16. Joy Wang, Architect, designer, founder of GlobalNomadWorld.com (Hong Hong)
  17. Konstantin Minin, Producer, composer (Russia)
